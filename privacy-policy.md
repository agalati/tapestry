# Privacy Policy

*Last updated April 2026*

## The short version

Tapestry stores everything on your device. We don't run servers, we don't create accounts, and we never see your data. Full stop.

## What we collect

Nothing. Tapestry has no backend, no analytics, and no telemetry. The app does not transmit any data over the internet.

Everything you add — people, moments, photos, voice recordings — lives exclusively in your device's local storage.

## Device backup

If you have enabled iCloud (iOS) or Google One (Android) device backup, your Tapestry data will be included in those backups automatically — because it lives in your app's standard storage directory.

We have no control over, or access to, those backup services. Their respective privacy policies govern that data.

## Third-party services

Tapestry uses the following third-party libraries bundled within the app itself. None of them receive your personal data:

- **Expo** — app runtime and build tools
- **React Native** — UI framework
- **expo-av** — local audio/video playback
- **expo-image-picker** — local photo selection
- **expo-notifications** — local (on-device) reminders only

No advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or analytics tools are included.

## Notifications

Reminders sent by Tapestry are scheduled locally on your device using iOS/Android notification APIs. No data leaves your device to trigger them.

## Children

Tapestry is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them. Because we collect nothing from anyone, this is straightforward.

## Changes to this policy

If we ever add features that involve data leaving your device, we will update this policy and surface a prominent notice in the app before any such change takes effect.
