## Common questions

### Is my data backed up?

Tapestry stores everything on your device. If you have iCloud (iOS) or Google One (Android) device backup enabled, your data is included automatically. We recommend keeping backup switched on.

### I deleted a moment — can I recover it?

Deletions are permanent within the app. If you have a device backup from before the deletion, you may be able to restore from it via your device's system settings.

### Can I transfer my data to a new phone?

Restoring from a device backup on your new phone will bring all your Tapestry data across. We're working on a dedicated export feature for a future update.

### The app is running slowly — what can I do?

Try force-quitting and reopening Tapestry. If you have a very large number of high-resolution photos, the app may take a moment to load. If problems persist, contact us and we'll investigate.

### I found a bug — how do I report it?

Please email us with a short description of what happened and what you were doing at the time. Screenshots are very helpful. We take all bug reports seriously.

### How do I delete all my data?

Go to **Settings → Reset all data**. This permanently removes everything from your device and cannot be undone.
